01. Ace: Project Management Website

Ace is a project management app inspired by Basecamp. It handles the four operations of CRUD, and uses Heroku Postgres as its database. It has a calendar component that allows users to add and view their upcoming events.


02. Coviz: Data Visualization Website

computer

Coviz is an interactive data visualization on the impact of COVID-19 over time in the United States. Its goal is to illustrate how the virus intersects with a variety of key issues, such as how it has affected jail and prison facilities across the U.S. The site uses D3 to render the main map and supplemental charts, such as a spike map, radial bar graph, and bubble chart. Data was pulled from sources such as the U.S. Census Bureau, the UCLA Bar Project, and Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
